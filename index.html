About Me

Building code by day,
trails by weekend.

Hey, I'm Shreekara Udupa — most people call me Shree. By profession I'm a developer, by passion I'm a wanderer who finds the best debug sessions happen at 12,000 feet above sea level.

I started this space because I got tired of the narrative that life only begins after you clock out on Friday. Every trek, every long ride, every late-night train to nowhere has taught me something a textbook couldn't.

"Beyond 9 to 5 Stories" is where I share those moments — raw, honest, and usually involving either extreme weather or questionable roadside food.

🏔 High-Altitude Trekker 🏍️ Biker ✈️ Travel Enthusiast 💻 Full-Stack Developer 📸 Amateur Photographer
